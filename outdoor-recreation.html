OUTDOOR RECREATION

Delivering excellence in recreation & relaxation

RECREATION EXCELLENCE

Quality recreation and leisure pursuits are an essential part of living a healthy and productive lifestyle. We believe in providing our guests with an abundance of choices including extraordinary recreation, leisure, and fitness opportunities.

Whether it's kayaking on the Potomac River, lacing up the skates to glide across the ice rinks in the Nation's Capital, taking a houseboat vacation on Lake Roosevelt in Washington state, enjoying a trail ride on horseback, or serving up an ace on a tennis court, we know you'll enjoy the high-quality experiences we provide at properties across the country.

SKIING

From early morning grooming to après-ski, we have 25 years of professional winter recreation experience. Operating a ski resort requires a solid understanding of snowmaking and maintenance procedures, and the focus on safety, environmental impacts, and promotion of the sport are essential to its growth. Guest Services executes high quality mountain activities creating an experience that builds skier loyalty.

  • Snowmaking and trail maintenance
  • Ski patrol and safety programs
  • Lift operations
  • Après-ski and lodge management
  • Ski school and lesson programs
  • Winter event coordination

GOLFING

Extending beyond the green, we have successfully managed championship golf courses including award-winning properties. Golf course management requires extensive experience and skill, and meticulous attention to detail—all of which Bright Wheeler Group provides.

  • Championship course management
  • Tee time optimization
  • Pro shop operations
  • Tournament coordination
  • Course maintenance
  • Membership programs

WATER RECREATION

From paddleboarding to kayaking to rowing to sailing, Bright Wheeler Group is deeply familiar with all facets of water sports. We manage and maintain water sport facilities and watercrafts, and we facilitate related camps, programs, lessons, and tours (scenic and educational). We take our stewardship of bodies of water seriously, implementing measures that ensure they remain clean and habitats enabled to nurture the wildlife they support.

  • Kayaking and paddleboarding
  • Rowing and sailing programs
  • Boat rentals and tours
  • Watercraft maintenance
  • Youth camps and lessons
  • Environmental stewardship
